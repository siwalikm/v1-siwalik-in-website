BIO

MY NAME IS SIWALIK MUKHERJEE and I’m a Graphic Designer and also a front-end Website developer. I have an inclination towards simple interfaces and minimal designs.

I'm also a big foodie and like most Indians, I too have a great affinity towards Biryani.

 

I’ve always loved great designs since I was a kid. So as I grew up, I shifted my interests from drawing and sketching to digital graphics and web designing.

 

I am currently pursuing my Computer Science Engineering degree from MVJCE, Bangalore.

 

Being an avid artist, I take up my graphic tablet or scetch book whenever I find some free time.

 

If you have any questions, feedback or just want to say hello, you can contact me at contact@siwalik.in.
